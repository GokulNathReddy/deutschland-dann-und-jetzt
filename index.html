German Cities: Past and Present

Germany's cities have been shaped by empires, wars, division, and reunification. This digital mini‑museum lets you explore how major cities—Berlin, Munich, Hamburg, Cologne, Frankfurt, Dresden, and Leipzig—looked in the past compared to today, while discovering their culture, food, and unique histories.

Use the interactive sliders on each city page to compare historical photographs with modern views, and follow the sections to learn about traditions, daily life, and iconic dishes.
